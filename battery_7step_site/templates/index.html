Critical Minerals

EV Battery Critical Mineral Supply Chain Analysis

A research-facing workspace for tracing mining, post-trade, refining, and cathode transitions across the EV battery supply chain. This viewer reads frozen outputs from trade_flow_opt and can switch between Original, First Optimization, and Second Optimization results without rerunning the flow solver.
